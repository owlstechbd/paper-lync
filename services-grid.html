PRODUCTS & SERVICES

Uncoated Paper

Uncoated paper does not have a coating to fill in between the fibers. It is rougher than coated paper and tends to be more porous, which makes it very absorbent

Coated Paper

paper coated by a mixture of materials or a polymer to impart certain qualities to the paper, including weight, surface gloss, smoothness, or reduced ink absorbency.

Cover

a strong durable printable paper of a type suitable for booklet or magazine covers

Text

flexible, can be easily rolled and folded and is used for printing flyers, handouts, letterheads, book pages, etc.

Copier Paper

8.5” x 11” – 75 Gsm - thin, lightweight and 75% opaque, and often has a matte coating for faster drying times.

Files/Folders

Medical Records: 11 pt Manila Letter Size, 14pt, 15 pt, 18pt – Color Paper - File folders usually consist of a sheet of heavy paper stock or other thin, but stiff, material which is folded in half, and are used to keep paper documents.

Legal Indexes

Avery Style, Standard Style, Blank Tabs, Letter only, Numbers only, Exhibit Letters, Exhibit Numbers, Miscellaneous, Collated, Uncollated - a compilation of law review and law-related articles organized by author, title, or subject.
